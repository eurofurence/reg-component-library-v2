# RadioCard

The `RadioCard` component combines the functionality of a `Card` and a `RadioItem`, making it possible
to have a group of cards in a `RadioGroup` of which 1 can be selected.

`RadioCard` components do _not_ adhere to the form grid. They are therefore also not required to be put
within a `Form` component, but the must be children of some `form` tag.
